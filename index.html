About Me

I am a post-doctoral fellow at Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR), Centre for Applicable Mathematics (CAM) Bangalore, India.

I am interseted in mathematical aspects of inverse problems related to partial differential dquations. My focus is on coefficient identification problems related to hyperbolic partial differential equations.
